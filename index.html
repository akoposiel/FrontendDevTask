Monthly Recurring Revenue

Monthly recurring revenue is the lifeblood of any SaaS business. It is calculated by adding up the value of all of your active subscription customers, minus any discounts. Increases in MRR come from three places: new customers, returning customers, and upgrading customers. Conversely, you can lose MRR to cancellations and downgrades.
